[DOC-ID: TRV-BUDGET-01] Finding Cheap Flights
Always search for flights using your browser's Incognito or private browsing mode. Airlines track your cookies and artificially raise prices if they see you checking the same route repeatedly. The cheapest days to fly domestically are typically Tuesdays and Wednesdays.

[DOC-ID: TRV-BUDGET-02] Currency Exchange Tricks
Never exchange physical cash at airport kiosks; they offer the worst exchange rates and charge hidden fees. Instead, use a local ATM in your destination city connected to a major bank to withdraw local currency. This ensures you get the real market exchange rate.
