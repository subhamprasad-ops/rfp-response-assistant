[DOC-ID: TRV-PACK-01] The Rolling Method vs Folding
To save up to 30% more space in your suitcase or backpack, roll your clothes tightly instead of folding them. Rolling also compresses the items and prevents deep wrinkles from forming during transit.

[DOC-ID: TRV-PACK-02] The Heavy Items Rule
Always place your heaviest items—such as shoes, jackets, and chargers—at the very bottom of your backpack or near the wheels of your suitcase. This keeps the center of gravity low, making your luggage significantly easier to carry or roll.
