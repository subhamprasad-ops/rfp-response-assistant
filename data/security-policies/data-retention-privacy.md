# Data Retention & Privacy Policy

**Policy ID:** SEC-PRV-05
**Owner:** Privacy & Legal
**Last Reviewed:** 2026-01-30

## Retention Periods

- **Active customer data:** retained for the duration of the customer's subscription.
- **Post-termination retention:** customer data is retained for 30 days after contract termination to allow for export or reactivation, then permanently deleted from production systems within 60 days total.
- **Backup retention:** encrypted backups are retained for 35 days on a rolling basis and are purged of deleted customer data on the same schedule.
- **Audit and security logs:** retained for 7 years to support compliance and forensic investigation needs.

## Data Deletion Requests

Customers may request deletion of specific records via API or the admin console at any time. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days and propagate to all backups within the standard backup retention window. A deletion certificate is available on request for compliance purposes.

## Data Minimization

Meridian Cloud collects only the data fields necessary to provide the contracted service. Optional telemetry (product usage analytics) can be disabled entirely at the workspace level by Enterprise customers.

## Employee Data Access

Meridian Cloud employees do not access customer data as part of normal operations. Support engineers may access customer data only with explicit customer consent granted through a support ticket, and all such access is logged and visible to the customer's workspace administrators.

## Data Portability

Customers can export their full dataset at any time in CSV, JSON, or via the export API, in a machine-readable format that supports migration to another provider.

## Customer Questions This Policy Typically Answers

- How long do you retain our data after we cancel?
- Can we request deletion of specific records?
- Do your employees have access to our data?
- Can we export our data if we leave?
- How long are logs retained?
