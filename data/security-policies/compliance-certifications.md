# Compliance & Certifications

**Policy ID:** SEC-CMP-03
**Owner:** GRC (Governance, Risk & Compliance)
**Last Reviewed:** 2026-05-20

## Certifications Held

- **SOC 2 Type II** — audited annually by an independent third party; report covers Security, Availability, and Confidentiality trust service criteria. Available under NDA.
- **ISO 27001:2022** — certified since 2023, covering our information security management system (ISMS).
- **ISO 27701** — privacy information management extension, certified 2024.
- **PCI DSS Level 1 Service Provider** — applicable to payment-processing components only.

## Regulatory Frameworks

- **GDPR** — Meridian Cloud acts as a data processor for customer data and maintains a Data Processing Addendum (DPA) with Standard Contractual Clauses for cross-border transfers.
- **CCPA/CPRA** — we do not sell personal data and support customer data subject access requests via API.
- **HIPAA** — Meridian Cloud will execute a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for customers on the Enterprise plan who process protected health information (PHI). PHI must be stored in workspaces with the HIPAA add-on enabled.
- **FedRAMP** — not currently authorized; on our public roadmap for FedRAMP Moderate, targeted 2027.

## Data Residency

Enterprise customers may pin data storage to a specific region: US (us-east-1, us-west-2), EU (eu-central-1), or APAC (ap-southeast-1). Data does not leave the selected region except for aggregated, de-identified product analytics.

## Subprocessors

A current list of subprocessors (cloud infrastructure, email delivery, customer support tooling, analytics) is published and updated with 30 days' advance notice before any new subprocessor is added. Customers may subscribe to subprocessor change notifications.

## Audit Rights

Enterprise customers may request a security questionnaire response, our latest SOC 2 Type II report, and penetration test summary. On-site audits are available for Enterprise contracts above a negotiated threshold, subject to reasonable scheduling and NDA.

## Customer Questions This Policy Typically Answers

- Are you SOC 2 certified? Can we see the report?
- Are you ISO 27001 certified?
- Do you support HIPAA / will you sign a BAA?
- Are you GDPR compliant? Do you offer a DPA?
- Can we choose where our data is stored (data residency)?
- Do you have a list of subprocessors?
- Are you FedRAMP authorized?
