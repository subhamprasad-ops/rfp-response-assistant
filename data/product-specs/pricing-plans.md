# Pricing & Plans

**Doc ID:** PROD-PRC-03
**Owner:** Revenue Operations
**Last Reviewed:** 2026-03-28

## Plan Tiers

- **Starter:** for small teams, up to 5 seats, 5 active workflows, community support. Billed monthly.
- **Business:** unlimited seats, unlimited workflows, SSO/SAML, 99.9% SLA, priority email support, standard connector library. Billed annually.
- **Enterprise:** everything in Business plus dedicated infrastructure options, HIPAA add-on, custom SLA (99.95%), SCIM provisioning, dedicated customer success manager, custom contract terms (MSA, DPA, security addenda), and volume-based connector/API pricing.

## Billing Model

Pricing is based on a combination of active seats and monthly workflow execution volume (data rows processed). Overage is billed at a published per-unit rate; Enterprise customers can pre-purchase execution volume at a discount.

## Contract Terms

Standard contracts are annual with net-30 payment terms. Enterprise contracts can be multi-year with custom payment schedules, and support purchase-order-based invoicing.

## Professional Services

Optional implementation packages are available: Quick Start (2-week guided onboarding), Migration Services (for customers moving from a competing platform), and ongoing Managed Services for customers who want Meridian Cloud to operate workflows on their behalf.

## Free Trial / POC

A 14-day free trial of the Business plan is available with no credit card required. For Enterprise evaluations, a scoped proof-of-concept (POC) with a dedicated solutions engineer can be arranged, typically 2–4 weeks, governed by a POC agreement.
