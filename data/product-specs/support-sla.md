# Customer Support & Service Levels

**Doc ID:** PROD-SUP-04
**Owner:** Customer Success
**Last Reviewed:** 2026-05-09

## Support Channels

- **Starter:** community forum and email support, best-effort response.
- **Business:** priority email and in-app chat support, target first response within 4 business hours.
- **Enterprise:** 24/7 phone, email, and chat support with a named Technical Account Manager (TAM) and dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM).

## Support Response SLA (Enterprise)

| Severity | First Response | Target Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| SEV-1 (production down) | 30 minutes | 4 hours |
| SEV-2 (major feature impaired) | 2 hours | 1 business day |
| SEV-3 (minor issue) | 1 business day | 5 business days |
| SEV-4 (question/enhancement) | 2 business days | best effort |

## Onboarding

Enterprise customers are assigned a dedicated Implementation team for the first 90 days, including a kickoff workshop, architecture review, and a shared success plan with milestones tracked jointly.

## Training & Documentation

Self-serve documentation, video tutorials, and a certification program (Meridian Certified Administrator) are available to all customers at no additional cost. Enterprise customers receive quarterly business reviews (QBRs) covering platform usage, roadmap alignment, and optimization recommendations.

## Escalation Path

Enterprise customers can escalate directly to their CSM or TAM, who can invoke an internal executive escalation process for unresolved SEV-1/SEV-2 issues beyond SLA targets.

## Customer Questions This Doc Typically Answers

- What support channels and hours are available?
- What is your support response SLA by severity?
- Do we get a dedicated account manager?
- What does onboarding look like?
- Is training/documentation included?
