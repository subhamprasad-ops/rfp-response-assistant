[DOC-ID: TRV-GEMS-01] Japan's Secret Bamboo Grove
While everyone visits the crowded Arashiyama Bamboo Grove in Kyoto, the lesser-known Muko Bamboo Forest located just outside the city offers a completely empty, peaceful alternative with identical towering green stalks.

[DOC-ID: TRV-GEMS-02] Italy's Hidden Water Village
Skip the overcrowded canals of Venice and visit Rasiglia, a tiny mountain village in Umbria. Waterways and miniature waterfalls run directly between old stone houses, making it feel like a fairy tale village without the tourist crowds.
